We interviewed Wesley about himself, his hobbies, and aspirations. Below are his responses.


1. What did you like most about growing up in Canton? -- The small town feel, how everyone knew everyone. The sense of community was strong.
2. Can you describe yourself in 3-5 words? -- Father, tall, kind.
3. What are you passionate about? -- My kids, UGA football.
4. How do you evaluate success? -- When all the moving parts come together for a common goal
5. What makes you unique? -- My beard.
6. What are your pet peeves? -- Slow people, bad coaching, poor spelling, small yappy dogs.
7. What do people most often criticize you about? -- Being too quiet/not talkative.
8. What do you look forward to most in the future? -- Traveling
9. Where are you most excited to travel to? -- Greece, no particular city
10. What do you wish the world did better? -- Communicate
