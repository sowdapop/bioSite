The Bearded Badass

We interviewed Wesley about himself, his hobbies, and aspirations. Below are his responses.

What did you like most about growing up in Canton?
The small town feel, how everyone knew everyone. The sense of community was strong.
Can you describe yourself in 3-5 words?
Father, tall, kind.
What are you passionate about?
My kids, UGA football.



How do you evaluate success?
When all the moving parts come together for a common goal
What makes you unique?
My beard.
What are your pet peeves?
Slow people, bad coaching, poor spelling, small yappy dogs.




What do people most often criticize you about?
Being too quiet/not talkative.
What do you look forward to most in the future?
Traveling
Where are you most excited to travel to?
Greece, no particular city
